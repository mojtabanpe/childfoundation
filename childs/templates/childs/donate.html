You are about to make an impact on a child’s life by making donation to Child Foundation UK.

Whether you decide to make a one-off donation to a specific child or to Child Foundation as a whole, we promise that the donation you make today will be used to keep a child at school or to keep their families safe and warm by addressing their urgent needs. We recommend you to Register / Log in before making donation which enables you manage your donations and helps us keep track of our donors and available funds. Regardless, your donation will change the lives of extremely vulnerable children across the country. It satisfies their urgent needs and helps them gain access to education and/or enables them remain in school.
