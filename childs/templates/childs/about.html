Our mission to help children in need, who have been identified as high achievers, remain in school.

Our Vision is that we believe that education can move a child beyond the stigma of poverty, and give that child the tools to reach his or her true and full potential.

UK Board of Directors

Shadi Khakpour – CF UK Chairman

I studied Computer Hardware Engineering in Iran, MBA-International Business in Malaysia and Germany and MSc. Project Management with Distinction in the UK. I have several years of working experience in marketing, technical sales, business and commercial analysis in IT and oil and gas industries. I have lived in different parts of the world and have been actively contributing to different charity organizations worldwide during the last decade. Now, I am very excited to be a member of Child Foundation family and help CF UK to prosper and support as many children as we can!

I believe there are no bad people, but good people who make bad decisions. The underlying reasons for many crimes and illegal activities are poverty, lack of education and emotional support since childhood. All children should have the right to learn, play, feel supported and loved, in order to be able to leave a positive legacy in the world. We, as adults, who have been blessed with family, safety, love, education and health, should do whatever we can, to create the same opportunities for beautiful souls who require assistance with all their basic needs. My most important ambition in life, is to help children reach their potentials by supporting them meet their basic needs and providing access to education. This cannot be possible, without the support of our kind sponsors, donors and volunteers. I truly believe that every SIMPLE ACT OF CARING CREATES AN ENDLESS RIPPLE! Together, we can make a difference!
